I created Mind Over Matteer (MOM) to help aid recovering athelthes with their journeys. Mind Over Matter is a unique, multidisciplinary facility dedicated to the comprehensive rehabilitation and mental well-being of athletes recovering from physical injuries. MOM's mission is to foster an environment that not only heals the body but also addresses the psychological challenges that often accompany injury recovery.

At Mind Over Matter the approach is to integrate physical rehabilitation with mental health support, understanding that the journey to recovery is as much psychological as it is physical. We believe that fostering a strong mindset is crucial to overcoming injury-related challenges and returning to peak performance. ur team of experts includes certified physical therapists, sports medicine doctors, and licensed psychologists, all of whom have extensive experience in their respective fields. They work collaboratively to create a personalized recovery plan for each athlete, encompassing physical therapy sessions, strength and conditioning training, and psychological counseling. Physical therapy at MOM is more than just regaining mobility. Our therapists use a variety of techniques to help athletes rebuild strength, improve flexibility, and restore function. They aim to not only expedite recovery but also to prevent future injuries through education about body mechanics and injury prevention strategies.

Alongside physical rehabilitation, our Mind Over Matter places a significant emphasis on mental health. We understand that injuries can lead to emotional distress, such as anxiety and depression, which can hinder the recovery process. Our mental health professionals provide therapeutic interventions to help athletes cope with these feelings, improve their mental resilience, and maintain a positive mindset throughout their recovery. Mind Over Matter also offers group therapy sessions and workshops where athletes can share their experiences and learn from others who are going through similar situations. In addition to the rehabilitation program, MOM provides nutritional guidance to optimize healing and recovery. Our nutritionists develop tailored meal plans to ensure that athletes receive the necessary nutrients to support their physical recovery and mental health.Our commitment to the athletes extends beyond their time at the institute. Post-recovery support is an integral part of our program, where we continue to provide guidance and resources to help athletes maintain their physical health and mental well-being even after they've returned to their sport.
